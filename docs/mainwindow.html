Worm Tank - Main Window

The main window shows the running simulation with and some statistics on previous generations.

The title of this window is changed to show what is currently running in the tank.

The top area contains information on the current run.
Generation The current generation being run
Tick The time in the current generation measured in ticks, where each tick is one move off all the worms.
Worms Left Number of worm alive in the tank
Food Left Amount of food left in the tank
Max energy The maximum energy any one worm has had

Under the information on the current run is the worm tank itself. This shows the worms wandering around the tank. The green dots are the food they eat and the longer moving coloured lines are the worms.

The worms grow and shrink in length depending on the amount of energy they have.

To see a key for the colours of the worms use the View menu, Worm list option.

The section to the right marked Leader stats gives information about the worm with the most energy at the end of the generation.

Age The number of generation the leader has been alive. i.e. The leader was in the top ten worms.
Leading for The number of generations the leader has been at the top at the end of the generation.
Ancestors This is a list of which worm won each generation, with the last generation on the left.

The section to the right marked Last generation stats gives information on the last finished generation.

Ticks The time the last generation lasted for
Food Left The amount of food left when the generation finished
Max energy The maximum energy any one worm has had in the last generation

The three line charts at the bottom summarize the history of the previous generations so trends can be seen.

The charts go from the oldest generation on the left to the last generation on the right. Two generations need to have passed before anything is shown.

Ticks The number of ticks the generations took
Food left The amount of food left at the end of the generations
Max Energy The maximum energy any one worm has had in the generations
